DEEP LEARNING · AUDIO

AudioCNN

End-to-end sound classification system

Designed and trained a ResNet-34-style CNN from scratch in PyTorch to classify raw audio into 50 environmental categories from the ESC-50 dataset. Built a DSP pipeline converting raw waveforms to 128-bin log-mel spectrograms, and optimized generalization with MixUp, SpecAugment, label smoothing, and a OneCycleLR schedule with AdamW.

PyTorch ResNet-34 ESC-50 log-mel spectrograms SpecAugment

COMPUTER VISION · TRANSFORMERS

Semantic Segmentation with SegFormer

Pixel-level understanding of urban scenes

Implemented a SegFormer-based model for pixel-level classification of urban street scenes on the Cityscapes dataset. Built a lightweight transformer encoder-decoder — a Mix Transformer backbone with an all-MLP decoder — to segment 19 semantic classes including roads, buildings, vehicles, and pedestrians, with a complete training pipeline from data loading to checkpoint management.
